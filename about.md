Hi there! I'm Eric, currently a lowly undergrad that has stumbled his way into all things mathematics, probability and game-theory related.

I started this blog to post all things interesting to me, free of expectations (pun not intended).

The goal is not to present polished academic treatments, but to work through cool problems, clean ideas, and useful heuristics in a way that is readable and honest.

I expect most posts to be short notes on probability, statistics, combinatorics, game theory and a sprinkle of code to put some ideas into production.

Some posts will be complete solutions to long problems. Others will just be partial explorations, computations, or derivations that are too nice to lose.

Either way, if you see anything here that's interesting, needs improvement, or just plain wrong - please shoot me an email at ericqian05@gmail.com. Otherwise, happy meandering!
